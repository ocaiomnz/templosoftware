# Template Software — Website Redesign Specification

> **Version:** 2.0  
> **Author:** Software Architecture Team  
> **Objective:** Transform the current website into a premium digital experience that reflects the technical excellence, engineering culture, and reliability of Template Software.

# 1. Vision

The website must stop looking like a marketing template and start looking like the digital presence of a high-end software engineering company.

Every pixel should reinforce three ideas:

- Trust
- Technical Excellence
- Business Value

We are not selling software.

We are selling solutions to complex business problems.
